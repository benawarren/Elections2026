## 2026 Detroit News Election Results

**Files:**
 - main.py: where it all comes together, execute to scrape results and add to Google Sheet
 - update_sheet.py: takes in scraped election data and adds to the specified Google Sheet
 - wayne_scraper.py: scrapes election results from Wayne County
 - macomb_scraper.py: scrapes election results from Macomb County
 - oakland_scraper.py: scrapes election results from Oakland County
